Tips & Tricks

  • Tip 1: In Hollow Knight, once you start you don't have much but your weapon. So to maximize use, try pogoing. Pogoing is where you downslash an enemy and use that hop you get from doing it to launch yourself to the next enemy or just in front of you.

  • Tip 2: In the first boss, you can skip the fight by breaking down the wall to your left.
